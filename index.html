About Me:

I'm a Portland, Oregon resident who is currently pursueing a career change from science to tech by attending a Javascript React course at Epicodus. I intend to focus my training on front-end web development but we'll see where my path leads me as I become more experienced. When I'm not busy coding I'm eating good things, drinking good cider, hiking/reading/yoga-ing, or hanging out with my husband and daughter.

Background:

I'm originally from California but I've lived in Oregon for so long I've been officially converted. I've spent the last 18 years in biomedical research under a number of contexts including autoimmune disease and orthopaedics. Although I enjoyed research immensely it is now time to move on and explore the world of web development.
